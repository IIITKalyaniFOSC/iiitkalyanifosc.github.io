PROJECTS

Project One

Our first project required us to build something that we could show to others as a demonstration of our skills with the various technologies.So we decided to build the official website of our FOSC which is hosted on GitHub and is being developed by a number of collaborators.

FOSC Website

Project Two

An annual logo design contest hosted on GitHub by the Algoholics (the Technical Committee), where the participants put on display their creativity in the form of a logo. This event will be conducted every year for the 1st year students as a test of their creativity where they will be guided by their seniors who will help them master the art of digital design and logo making.

Project Three

An attempt to create a paper-vault containing all the question papers of the semester examinations, an approach to help juniors in better preparation for their exams and also to encourage contribution.
