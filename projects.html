Project Two

An annual logo design contest hosted on GitHub by the Algoholics (the Technical Committee), where the participants put on display their creativity in the form of a logo. This event will be conducted every year for the 1st year students as a test of their creativity where they will be guided by their seniors who will help them master the art of digital design and logo making.
